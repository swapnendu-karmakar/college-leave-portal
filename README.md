# College Leave Portal

A comprehensive and intuitive web application designed to streamline the leave management process across a multi-tiered educational institution. The system efficiently connects students, faculty, and administrators through a unified portal tailored to simplify day-to-day administrative tasks.

## Overall Description

The College Leave Portal eliminates the friction of traditional paper-based or disjointed communication systems for requesting and approving absences. By modeling the intricate educational hierarchy—spanning colleges, departments, branches, and specific divisions—the platform ensures that leave requests always route to the appropriate mentor, Head of Department, or administrative level. 

With dedicated interfaces for each specialized role, users can quickly submit applications, monitor statuses in real-time, and maintain a clear, transparent record of all historical requests. Furthermore, its automated administrative features allow organization managers to bulk onboard entire classrooms or faculties seamlessly, ensuring the portal is easy to adopt and maintain at scale.
